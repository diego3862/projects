Partnership Collaboration
AG Strategy & Partners has collaboration agreements in place with our Partner specialist firms along the continuum of Strategy Development, Business Development and Implementation.


In parallel, we have access to the FinTech ecosystem, so being able to reach both specialists across the FinTech Core Competences (i.e. Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Blockchain, APIs and Cloud) and a wide array of FinTech start-ups that provide specific solutions across a large variety of client segments and products in the Financial Services industry.


For any client project, we create an Agile Team formed by multi-functional specialists, external and internal to the client, ensuring a cost-effective delivery tailored to the problem at hand.
