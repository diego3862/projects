History & Partner Firms

AG Strategy & Partners was founded by Dr. Angel Gavieiro, former Head of Strategy for Lloyds Bank and Barclays Bank, and McKinsey alumni, in Abu Dhabi (UAE) in July 2015; initially launched as an independent brand operating within the umbrella of a local IT consulting & implementation firm (Crest Global, Masdar City), and subsequently was incorporated in London in May 2017.

AG Strategy & Partners maintains a close association with other 6 specialized Partner firms along the continuum of Strategy Development, Business Development (both Organic and Inorganic) and Change & Implementation.

  • 10EQS: global professional crowdsourcing of expertise and creativity to generate innovative business solutions for leading organizations.

  • Umbrex: global community of independent top-tier strategy consultants (i.e. McKinsey & Co., BCG and Bain & Co.).

  • Shivam Advisors: advisory services to financial institutions and financial technology companies.

  • Fraser Coutts & Partners: advisory services in corporate finance, economics, real estate and private assets broking.

  • Tori Global: change & transformation to financial institutions based on high level industry experience.

  • Arimon Consulting: digital & analytics to enable insight-driven profitable growth.
