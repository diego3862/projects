Hollistic Management Framework
The Holistic Management Framework is a 10-framework set that ensures coherence & consistency for both the Vertical axis of Group, Division and Business Units, as well as the Horizontal axis of New and/or Existing Geographies and Businesses.

It can be applied as the main strategy governance policy for the organization, either to a complex Group with several divisions or to a single-market company or business unit in isolation.
